Mechanical Engineer Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a professional mechanical engineer

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 11-12 (PCM)

Take Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics with minimum 60% marks.

Core Subjects
Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Entrance Exams
JEE Main, BITSAT, State CETs
2

B.Tech/BE in Mechanical Engineering

4-year undergraduate program from recognized institution.

Year 1
Engineering Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Engineering Mechanics
Year 2
Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Strength of Materials
Year 3
Machine Design, Heat Transfer, Manufacturing Processes
Year 4
CAD/CAM, Robotics, Project Work, Industrial Training
3

Software Skills

Learn essential software tools for mechanical engineering.

CAD Software
AutoCAD, SolidWorks, CATIA, Creo
Analysis Tools
ANSYS, MATLAB, Simulink
Programming
Python, C++, MATLAB
4

Specializations

Choose your area of interest for career growth.

Design
Product Design, CAD, Finite Element Analysis
Thermal
HVAC, Power Plants, Refrigeration
Manufacturing
Production, CNC, Automation, Quality Control
Automotive
Vehicle Design, Engine Systems, Electric Vehicles
5

Internships & Placements

Gain industry experience and secure job offers.

Core Companies
Tata Motors, Mahindra, L&T, BHEL, Hyundai
PSUs
BHEL, IOCL, GAIL, NTPC (through GATE)
IT/Consulting
Infosys, TCS, Accenture (for non-core roles)
6

Higher Studies & Certifications

Advanced options for career growth.

M.Tech
Specialization in chosen field (through GATE)
MBA
Operations, Supply Chain, Project Management
Certifications
Six Sigma, PMP, Lean Manufacturing

Quick Info

  • Duration: 4 years (B.Tech)
  • Entry Exams: JEE Main, BITSAT, State CETs
  • Course Fees: ₹1-8 Lakhs (Govt), ₹3-12 Lakhs (Private)
  • Starting Salary: ₹3-8 LPA

Top Colleges

  • IIT Bombay - Mechanical
  • IIT Delhi - Mechanical
  • IIT Madras - Mechanical
  • NIT Trichy - Mechanical
  • BITS Pilani - Mechanical

Key Skills

AutoCAD SolidWorks ANSYS MATLAB Thermodynamics Manufacturing
