Complete Roadmap

1

Class 12 Foundation

Build strong foundation in core subjects.

Mathematics
Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry
English
Grammar, Vocabulary, Comprehension
General Knowledge
Current Affairs, History, Geography, Polity
2

Graduation

Complete graduation in any discipline.

Eligibility
Any graduation degree (Minimum 50-60% varies by post)
Age Limit
18-32 years (with relaxations for categories)
3

Tier 1 - Preliminary Exam

First stage - objective type, screening test.

Sections
Reasoning, Maths, English, General Awareness
Questions
100 questions, 200 marks
Duration
60 minutes
4

Tier 2 - Mains Exam

Second stage - objective type, merit deciding.

Paper 1
Quantitative Abilities (200 marks)
Paper 2
English Language (200 marks)
Paper 3
Statistics (200 marks) - for specific posts
Paper 4
General Studies (200 marks) - for specific posts
5

Tier 3 - Descriptive Test

Third stage - pen and paper descriptive exam.

Format
Essay, Letter, Precis, Application writing
Duration
60 minutes
Marks
100 marks
6

Tier 4 - Skill Test/Computer Proficiency

Final stage - qualifying in nature.

Data Entry
8,000 key depressions per hour for Tax Assistants
Computer Proficiency
Word, Excel, PowerPoint for certain posts
7

Posts & Salary through SSC CGL

Various Group B and C posts in government departments.

₹45,000-55,000/month (Level 7)
Sub-Inspector (CBI)
₹45,000-55,000/month
Statistical Officer
₹45,000-55,000/month
Departments
Income Tax, Customs, CAG, MEA, CBDT, CBIC

  • Preparation Time: 6-12 months

  • CBI
