Our Story

This Student Roadmap Website has been developed as a final-year project by diploma 6th-semester students with the goal of helping learners make informed decisions about their future. Understanding how confusing it can be to choose the right path after 10th, 12th, or diploma, we created a platform that provides easy-to-understand roadmaps and guidance to explore strengths and interests. Our project reflects our commitment to using technology to support students like us, offering a simple, reliable, and helpful resource built with real student experiences in mind.
