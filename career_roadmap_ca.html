Chartered Accountant Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a CA in India

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 12 (Any Stream)

Complete 12th grade with any stream. Commerce is preferred but not mandatory.

Commerce Stream
Accounts, Economics, Business Studies (Recommended)
Other Streams
Science/Arts also eligible with minimum 50% marks
2

CA Foundation (4 Months)

Register with ICAI and appear for Foundation exam after 12th.

Papers
4 papers: Accounts, Law, Maths, Economics
Passing
50% in aggregate, minimum 40% in each paper
3

CA Intermediate (8 Months)

After Foundation, register for Intermediate. Can also register after graduation (Direct Entry).

Group 1
Accounting, Corporate Law, Taxation
Group 2
Costing, Auditing, Financial Management
Both Groups
Pass both groups separately or together
4

Articleship (3 Years)

Practical training under a practicing CA while studying for Final.

Training Areas
Audit, Taxation, Accounting, Consultancy
Stipend
₹1,000-5,000 per month (increases with tenure)
5

CA Final (During Articleship)

Appear for Final exams in the last 6 months of articleship.

Group 3
Financial Reporting, Strategic Finance, Advanced Auditing
Group 4
Corporate Laws, Advanced Tax, Electives
6

Membership & Certificate of Practice

After passing Final, enroll as ICAI member and get COP to practice.

7

Career Paths & Earnings

Choose your career path based on your interests.

Practice
Own firm (₹5-10 LPA starting, unlimited potential)
Industry
Corporate roles in MNCs (₹7-12 LPA starting)
Big 4
Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG (₹8-15 LPA)
Banking/Finance
Investment banks, NBFCs (₹10-20 LPA)

Quick Info

  • Duration: 4.5 years (Foundation route)
  • Exams: Foundation, Intermediate, Final
  • Course Fees: ₹40,000-60,000 (Total ICAI fees)
  • Starting Salary: ₹6-12 LPA

Top Recruiters

  • Deloitte
  • PwC
  • EY
  • KPMG
  • Goldman Sachs
  • Tata Group

Key Skills
