Dentist (BDS) Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a dentist in India

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 11-12 (PCB)

Take Physics, Chemistry, Biology with English. Minimum 50% marks required.

Core Subjects
Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English
Minimum Marks
50% in PCB (40% for reserved categories)
2

Crack NEET-UG

Qualify NEET with good rank for admission to dental colleges.

Eligibility
17 years old, 12th pass with PCB
Exam Pattern
180 questions, 3 hours, 720 marks
3

BDS (5 Years)

Bachelor of Dental Surgery - 4 years academic + 1 year compulsory internship

Year 1
General Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Dental Anatomy
Year 2
General Pathology, Microbiology, Dental Materials, Preclinical Dentistry
Year 3
General Medicine, General Surgery, Oral Pathology, Pharmacology
Year 4
Oral Medicine, Orthodontics, Periodontics, Pedodontics, Prosthodontics
4

Compulsory Internship (1 year)

Rotatory internship in various dental departments.

Clinical Training
Oral Surgery, Orthodontics, Prosthodontics, Periodontics
Community Dentistry
Dental camps, Public health programs
5

Registration with Dental Council

Register with Dental Council of India or State Dental Council to practice.

6

Post-graduation (MDS) - Optional

3 years specialization. Appear for NEET-MDS.

Clinical Specialties
Orthodontics, Oral Surgery, Periodontics, Prosthodontics
Diagnostic Specialties
Oral Pathology, Oral Medicine, Public Health Dentistry
7

Career Paths & Earnings

Choose your career path based on your qualifications.

Private Practice
Own clinic (₹5-15 LPA starting, increases with time)
Government Sector
Government hospitals, PHCs (₹6-10 LPA)
Private Hospitals
Corporate hospitals, Dental chains (₹4-8 LPA)
Academic/Research
Dental colleges, Research institutes (₹5-9 LPA)

Quick Info

  • Duration: 5 years (BDS) + 3 years (MDS)
  • Entry Exams: NEET-UG, NEET-MDS
  • Course Fees: ₹2-10 Lakhs (Govt), ₹15-40 Lakhs (Private)
  • Starting Salary: ₹4-8 LPA (Job), ₹5-15 LPA (Private Practice)

Key Skills

Manual Dexterity Attention to Detail Patient Communication Diagnostic Skills Stamina
