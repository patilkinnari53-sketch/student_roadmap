Multimedia
Photography, Video editing, Audio production
Digital
Social media, SEO, Content management

Internships & Experience

Print Media
Newspapers, Magazines - Times of India, Hindu
Digital Media
News portals, YouTube channels, Blogs
Broadcast
TV channels - NDTV, CNN-News18, Republic

Specializations

Choose your area of expertise.

Cover politics, parliament, elections
Business
Markets, economy, corporate news
Sports
Sports events, athlete interviews
Entertainment
Bollywood, celebrity news, reviews
