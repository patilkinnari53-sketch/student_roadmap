Fashion Designer Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a fashion designer in India

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 12 (Any Stream)

Complete 12th grade. Fine Arts or Commerce with creative subjects preferred.

2

Entrance Exams Preparation

Prepare for fashion design entrance exams.

NIFT Entrance
National Institute of Fashion Technology
NID DAT
National Institute of Design
CEED
Common Entrance Exam for Design
State CETs
Various state-level design entrance tests
3

Undergraduate Programs

Choose the right fashion design program.

B.Des Fashion
4 years - Bachelor of Design in Fashion
B.Sc Fashion Design
3 years - Science-based fashion program
Diploma
1-2 years - Specialized fashion diplomas
4

Core Curriculum

Learn essential fashion design skills.

Design Basics
Illustration, Pattern Making, Draping, Garment Construction
Technical Skills
CAD for Fashion, Textile Science, Pattern Grading
Fashion Business
Fashion Marketing, Merchandising, Brand Management
5

Internships & Industry Exposure

Gain practical experience in the fashion industry.

Design Houses
Work with established fashion designers
Apparel Brands
Intern with clothing brands and retailers
Fashion Weeks
Assist during fashion week events
6

Career Paths & Earnings

Fashion design offers diverse career opportunities.

Fashion Designer
Create original clothing lines (₹3-8 LPA starting)
Textile Designer
Design fabrics and prints (₹3-6 LPA)
Fashion Stylist
Style for shoots, celebrities (₹4-10 LPA)
Fashion Merchandiser
Retail buying and planning (₹4-8 LPA)
Own Label
Start your own fashion brand (Income varies)

Quick Info

  • Duration: 3-4 years (Degree)
  • Entrance Exams: NIFT, NID, UCEED, CEED
  • Course Fees: ₹2-10 Lakhs (NIFT), ₹1-5 Lakhs (Private)
  • Starting Salary: ₹3-7 LPA

Top Institutes

  • NID, Ahmedabad
