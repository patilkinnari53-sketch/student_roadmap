Psychologist Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a psychologist in India

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 11-12 (Any Stream)

Complete 12th grade. Psychology as a subject is beneficial but not mandatory.

2

Bachelor's Degree (3 Years)

Choose the right undergraduate program.

BA Psychology
3 years - Arts stream, focus on theoretical psychology
B.Sc Psychology
3 years - Science stream, includes research methods
BA Applied Psychology
3 years - Focus on practical applications
3

Master's Degree (2 Years)

MA/M.Sc in Psychology - essential for professional practice.

Clinical Psychology
Mental health assessment and therapy
Counseling Psychology
Guidance and counseling in educational/org settings
Industrial Psychology
HR, organizational behavior, workplace psychology
Child Psychology
Child development, pediatric mental health
4

M.Phil/PhD (2-3 Years)

Advanced specialization required for clinical practice.

M.Phil Clinical
2 years - Required for RCI registration as Clinical Psychologist
PhD
3-5 years - For research and academic careers
5

Internship & Training

Practical training under supervision.

Clinical Internship
1 year at hospitals, mental health clinics
Supervised Practice
Work under licensed psychologists
6

Registration with RCI

Register with Rehabilitation Council of India to practice as a clinical psychologist.

7

Career Paths & Earnings

Psychology offers diverse career opportunities.

Clinical Psychologist
Hospitals, clinics, private practice (₹4-8 LPA starting)
Counselor
Schools, colleges, NGOs (₹3-6 LPA)
Industrial Psychologist
Corporate HR, training (₹5-10 LPA)
Academic/Researcher
Universities, research institutes (₹4-8 LPA)
Private Practice
Self-employed (₹500-2000 per session)

Quick Info

  • Duration: 5-7 years (BA + MA + M.Phil)
  • Entrance Exams: CUET-PG, JMI, BHU, DU Entrance
  • Course Fees: ₹30,000-3 Lakhs (Govt), ₹2-8 Lakhs (Private)
  • Starting Salary: ₹3-6 LPA

Top Institutes

  • Delhi University (DU)
  • Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi
  • BHU, Varanasi
  • TISS, Mumbai
  • Christ University, Bangalore

Key Skills

Empathy Active Listening Patience Ethical Judgment Research
