Pharmacist Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a licensed pharmacist in India

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 11-12 (PCB)

Take Physics, Chemistry, Biology with English. Minimum 50% marks required.

Core Subjects
Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English
Minimum Marks
50% in PCB (40% for reserved categories)
2

Choose Your Path

Select the right pharmacy program based on your career goals.

Diploma (D.Pharm)
2 years, can start pharmacy business or work as pharmacist
Bachelor (B.Pharm)
4 years, better career opportunities and higher salary
Direct Entry
After B.Sc, 2 years B.Pharm (lateral entry)
3

D.Pharm (2 Years)

Diploma in Pharmacy - basic qualification to practice as a pharmacist.

Year 1
Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmacognosy
Year 2
Hospital Pharmacy, Clinical Pharmacy, Drug Store Management
Internship
500 hours practical training in hospital/retail pharmacy
4

B.Pharm (4 Years) - Optional

Bachelor of Pharmacy for advanced career opportunities.

Year 1-2
Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutics
Year 3-4
Medicinal Chemistry, Pharmacy Practice, Biotechnology
Project Work
Research project in final year
5

Pharmacy Council Registration

Register with State Pharmacy Council to practice legally.

Documents
Degree certificate, internship proof, identity proof
License
Receive pharmacist license to practice
6

Career Paths & Earnings

Choose your career path based on your qualifications and interests.

Retail Pharmacy
Own store or work at medical store (₹2-5 LPA)
Hospital Pharmacist
Work in government/private hospitals (₹3-6 LPA)
Pharmaceutical Industry
Production, QA/QC, R&D (₹4-8 LPA)
Higher Studies
M.Pharm, Pharm.D, PhD (₹5-12 LPA after PG)

Quick Info

  • Duration: 2 years (D.Pharm) or 4 years (B.Pharm)
  • Entry Exams: GPAT, State CETs, Merit-based
  • Course Fees: ₹20,000-1 Lakh (D.Pharm), ₹40,000-3 Lakhs (B.Pharm)
  • Starting Salary: ₹2-5 LPA

Top Colleges

  • Jamia Hamdard, Delhi
  • BITS Pilani - Pharmacy
  • Manipal College of Pharmacy
  • NIPER, Mohali
  • Poona College of Pharmacy, Pune

Key Skills

Drug Knowledge Patient Counseling Inventory Management Attention to Detail Communication
