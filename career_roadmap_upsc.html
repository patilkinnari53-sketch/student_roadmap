Civil Services (UPSC) Roadmap

Complete guide to cracking UPSC CSE and becoming an IAS/IPS/IFS officer

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 11-12 Foundation

Build strong foundation in basic subjects regardless of stream.

Humanities
History, Geography, Polity, Economics (Natural advantage)
Science
Good for optional subjects like Math, Physics, etc.
Commerce
Strong in Economics, Accountancy for commerce optional
2

Graduation Degree (Any Stream)

Complete graduation in any discipline from recognized university.

Eligibility
Any graduation degree (BA, B.Sc, B.Com, B.Tech, etc.)
Minimum Marks
No minimum percentage required, just pass
Age Limit
21-32 years (with relaxations for categories)
3

UPSC CSE Prelims

First stage of the examination - objective type.

Paper 1
General Studies (History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science, Environment)
Paper 2
CSAT - Aptitude (Qualifying - 33% needed)
Preparation
6-12 months intensive study with NCERTs and standard books
4

UPSC CSE Mains

Second stage - descriptive/written examination.

Compulsory Papers
Essay, GS 1-4 (4 papers)
Optional Subject
2 papers on chosen optional subject
Language Papers
English + one Indian language (qualifying)
Total Papers
9 papers, 1750 marks
5

Personality Test (Interview)

Final stage - face-to-face interview with UPSC board.

Marks
275 marks
Assessment
Personality, communication, leadership, current affairs
Preparation
Mock interviews, current affairs, DAF analysis
6

Optional Subjects for UPSC

Choose your optional wisely based on interest and scoring potential.

Popular Options
Geography, History, Public Admin, Sociology, Political Science
Science Options
Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology
Language Options
Hindi, Sanskrit, English, Urdu, etc.
7

Career Paths & Salary

Top civil services and their career progression.

IAS
Administrative Service - DM, Secretary, Cabinet Secretary
IPS
Police Service - SP, DIG, IG, DGP
IFS
Foreign Service - Ambassador, High Commissioner
Central Services
IRS, IRTS, IPoS, etc.
Salary
Starting ₹56,000/month + allowances, highest pay scales in government

Quick Info

  • Preparation Time: 1-2 years typically
  • Eligibility: Graduation, 21-32 years
  • Coaching Fees: ₹50,000-2 Lakhs (Optional)
  • Starting Salary: ₹56,000/month + perks
