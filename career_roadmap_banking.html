Banking (IBPS/SBI) Roadmap

Complete guide to cracking banking exams and becoming a PO/Clerk

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 12 Foundation

Build strong foundation in Mathematics and English.

Mathematics
Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Data Interpretation
English
Grammar, Vocabulary, Comprehension
Commerce
Accounts, Economics (helpful but not mandatory)
2

Graduation

Complete graduation in any discipline from recognized university.

Eligibility
Any graduation degree (Minimum 50-60% varies by exam)
Age Limit
20-30 years for PO, 20-28 years for Clerk
Preferred Streams
B.Com, BBA, BA Economics, B.Sc Mathematics
3

Preliminary Exam

First stage - objective type, qualifying in nature.

Sections
English, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning Ability
Duration
1 hour
Marks
100 marks
4

Mains Exam

Second stage - objective + descriptive for some exams.

Sections
Reasoning, Maths, English, GA, Computer Knowledge
Descriptive
Essay, Letter Writing, Precis (for some exams)
Duration
2-3 hours
5

Interview & Language Test

Final stage for PO exams, language test for Clerk.

Interview
100 marks for PO, assesses personality and banking knowledge
Language Test
Local language proficiency (qualifying)
6

Major Banking Exams

Different exams for different positions and banks.

IBPS PO
Probationary Officer in public sector banks
IBPS Clerk
Clerical cadre in public sector banks
SBI PO
PO in State Bank of India (prestigious)
SBI Clerk
Junior Associate in SBI
RBI Grade B
Officer in Reserve Bank of India (highest level)
7

Career Path & Salary

Banking offers stable career with good growth.

Clerk
Starting ₹20,000-25,000/month
PO
Starting ₹40,000-50,000/month
Promotions
Assistant Manager → Manager → Chief Manager → GM
Perks
House rent, medical, travel, pension, loans at concessional rates

Quick Info

  • Preparation Time: 6-12 months
  • Eligibility: Graduation, 20-30 years
  • Coaching Fees: ₹15,000-50,000
  • Starting Salary: ₹4-6 LPA (Clerk), ₹7-9 LPA (PO)

  • Punjab National Bank (PNB)
  • Bank of Baroda
  • Canara Bank
  • RBI, NABARD, SIDBI
