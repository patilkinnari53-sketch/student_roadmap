Company Secretary Roadmap

Complete guide to becoming a CS in India

Complete Roadmap

1

Class 12 (Any Stream)

Complete 12th grade with any stream. Minimum 50% marks required.

2

CSEET (4 Months)

Company Secretary Executive Entrance Test - mandatory for entry.

Papers
Business Communication, Legal Aptitude, Economic Reasoning, Current Affairs
Exemption
Graduates/Post-graduates exempted (Direct Entry)
3

CS Executive (1 Year)

Register for Executive program after passing CSEET.

Module 1
Jurisprudence, Company Law, Accounting, Corporate Governance
Module 2
Capital Markets, Tax Laws, Economic, Commercial Laws
4

CS Professional (1.5 Years)

Advanced level with 3 modules and 9 papers.

Module 1
Governance, Secretarial Audit, Corporate Restructuring
Module 2
Taxation, Financial Treasury, Insolvency, Ethics
Module 3
Drafting, Appearances, Pleadings, Electives
5

Training (15 Months)

Practical training during Professional course.

Training Period
15 months under practicing CS or in company
Stipend
₹3,000-8,000 per month
6

Membership & Career

After passing Professional, enroll as ICSI member.

Practice
Independent practice as CS (₹5-8 LPA starting)
Employment
Corporate roles in companies (₹6-10 LPA)
Combination
CS + CA/CMA/Law (₹10-15 LPA)

Quick Info

  • Duration: 3-4 years
  • Exams: CSEET, Executive, Professional
  • Course Fees: ₹40,000-50,000 (Total ICSI fees)
  • Starting Salary: ₹5-8 LPA
