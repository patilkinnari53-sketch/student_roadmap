Services & Academies

Physical Standards

  • Height: 157 cm (Army), 165 cm (IAF)
  • Chest: 77 cm (min) with 5 cm expansion
  • Weight: Proportionate to height
  • Vision: 6/6 (better), 6/9 (worse) correctable
